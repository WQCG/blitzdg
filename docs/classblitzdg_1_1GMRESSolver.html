Detailed Description

Preconditioned, restarted GMRES with Modified Gram-Schmidt orthogonalization.

Solves the right-preconditioned linear system , where and is the preconditioner.

Stopping Criteria

The solver iterates until one of the following criteria are met:

  • The convergence criterion is met: The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::success.

  • The divergence criterion is met: The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::diverged.

  • The stagnation criterion is met: The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::stagnation.

  • The maximum number of iterations is reached. The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::maxits.

The norm is always the vector 2-norm and is the unpreconditioned residual of the th iterate .

Various other factors may cause the solver to terminate. These include:

  • The input matrix or the preconditioner are singular. The solver termintes with convergence flag ConvFlag::breakdown.

  • The residual norm is inf or nan. The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::inf_or_nan.

  • The matrix-vector multiply fails. The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::matvec_fail.

  • Application of the preconditioner fails. The solver terminates with convergence flag ConvFlag::precon_fail.
